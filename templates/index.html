Important Crop Information for Farmers

Wheat

Wheat grows best in cool climates and well-drained loamy soil. Requires moderate water and full sunlight.

Rice

Rice needs high rainfall or proper irrigation. Grows best in clay soil and warm temperatures.

Sugarcane

Sugarcane requires a long growing season, high temperature, and fertile well-drained soil.

Cotton

Cotton grows well in black soil and requires warm climate with low to moderate rainfall.

Soybean

Soybean prefers warm weather, well-drained soil, and moderate rainfall.

Maize

Maize requires plenty of sunlight, fertile soil, and moderate irrigation.
